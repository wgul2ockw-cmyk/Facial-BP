ABOUT v9

ROI Method

43 landmark patches (Elgendi et al. 2024, npj Biosensing): 10 forehead + 18 left cheek + 15 right cheek. 20×20 pixel patches, RGB 5-230 threshold (pyVHR). No skin-color segmentation needed.

Face Alignment

Elliptical guide with real-time checks: face size (25-70%), centering (±12%), yaw (<15°), pitch (neutral). Must pass all checks for 30 consecutive frames before scanning begins.

Signal Processing

POS algorithm (Wang et al. 2017): 1.6s windows, 50% overlap, Hanning-windowed overlap-add. 3rd-order Butterworth bandpass 0.7-3.0 Hz (zero-phase). SNR computed via de Haan method; segments below 0 dB rejected.

Models

GBR (300+300 trees) + NN (28→256→128→64→32→2) ensemble. Trained on 1,638 samples from 275 subjects. SBP MAE 3.29, DBP MAE 2.39 mmHg.
